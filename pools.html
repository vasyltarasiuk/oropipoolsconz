Oropi Hot Pools & Café

Main Pool

The Oropi Hot Pools have great affordable pool prices!

From the pool you can enjoy the relaxing temperatures ranging between 28-30 degrees celcius (summer) and 34-36 degrees celcius (winter).

Our hot water comes from a bore on-site that is 400mtrs deep.

It is safe to put your head under water.

Come, relax & enjoy the beautiful scenery surrounding the pool. You can even purchase gift vouchers which make perfect presents for parents, children and other family members!

Enjoy yourself at the Oropi Hot Pools & Café today!

To book call us on 07 543 3913 or email info@oropipools.co.nz.




Private Pools

Private Hot Spa Pool

Our private indoor spas are the perfect way to relax and wind down.

All private pools have their own ensuite with temperature kept at 38-39 degrees celsius all year round.

We have an open roof pool so you can sit and relax while star gazing! Please request this pool on booking if you would like it.

Ideal for Valentines night, birthdays or anniversary special moments.

To book call us on 07 543 3913 or email info@oropipools.co.nz.
